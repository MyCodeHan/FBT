TRECC is about Community Development


"We Support Families & Communities Based on Their Specific Needs."

Your journey is on TRECC
to start right here in Chicago!

TRECC (Technology Renewable Energy Command Centers) the community development foundation in partnership with Fbrands, LLC announces FREE PROGRAMS accessible to community members who need support navigating unemployment, community development, and violence prevention. The goal of this programming initiative is to actively support people with sustainability resources as they move towards a more enriched quality of life. We do this through Technology Workforce Certifications & Job Placement, Renewable Energy Workforce Development, Civic Leadership, Career Readiness Expos and Mentorship.

PROGRAMS

TRECC EXPOS

Renewable Energy
Workforce Development Expo

Renewable Energy Workforce Development Expo: A comprehensive program that exposes youth, adults, and participants for a career in the renewable industry field with a target focus on energy efficiency, air source, geothermal heat pumps, solar, wind, and batteries. Participants will have an up close in person experience learning from field experts and professionals. Certification and Job Placement opportunities available for interested participants.

Technology Workforce Certifications
& Job Placement Expo

Technology Workforce Certifications & Job Placement Expo: A continuous 3-month technology certification program that prepares youth, adults, and participants to earn at minimum $60k and up salary with benefits with a top ten fortune 500 company upon completion. Participants will learn basics of computer hardware, software, cyber security and emerging technologies.

  • No Comments

Civic Leadership Expo

Civic Leadership Expo: Program participants will learn critical principles to community building, foster creativity, and develop leadership skills. Participants will be exposed to the various levels of government in the U.S. and its impact on their day-to-day lives. They will be challenged to think critically about leadership concepts, traditions, and ways to improve their communities through active engagement.

Career Readiness Expo

Career Readiness Expo: Program participants will be introduced to high paying career fields. They will learn of the certifications needed to position themselves for these salaried focused careers with an attractive benefits package.
