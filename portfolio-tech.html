TECHNOLOGY CAREER EXPO & TRAINING

Our Goals

We Recruit, Educate, Mentor and Place Community Participants in Entry Level Jobs Provide on-going Career Coaching, Life Skills and Personal Support for Success. Build a strong, resilient, and equitable recovery by making investments that support long-term growth and opportunities for those interested in Tech Careers.

Career Exposure

Students will participate in engaging activities where professional skills will be taught and measured by a combination of group projects, computer laboratory experiments and fun activities.

Career Jobs that pay over 60K

  • Computer Support Specialist (Help Desk)
  • Computer Systems Administrator
  • Web Developer (Web Design)
  • Information Security Analyst (Cyber Security)
  • Software Developer (Programmer)
  • Cloud Architect (Cloud Practitioner)

Training Specifications

We provide ongoing 12- 16 week training program focused on preparing participants for entry level technology support position and specializing in the following skill sets:

  • Hardware computers (PC) configuration, support, and maintenance
  • IT infrastructure networking, virtualization, storage, and cloud architecture IT infrastructure Security
  • System and Network Troubleshooting
  • Documentation and ticketing procedures
  • IT call center0 customer service and support
  • Computer and Mobile Device Build and Repair
  • Web Design using Google UX software and tools

Live instruction will take place monthly, with an approximate duration of 3 hours per meeting. Students will perform additional hands-on activities reinforcing the technical concepts. Specific dates and times will be mutually agreed upon based on administrative requirements, instructor schedule, and student availability.

Student skill sets will be measured with a combination of exams, and laboratory experiments. Students will also participate in our entrepreneurial training program where they will earn money based on skills and services provided to our community stakeholders.

● Self-awareness ● Critical thinking ● Decision Making ● Problem Solving ● Effective Communication ● Interpersonal Relationships ● Empathy ● Coping with Stress ● Coping with emotions ● Resume writing ● Financial Literacy ● Setting goals ● Entrepreneurship ● Self Love
