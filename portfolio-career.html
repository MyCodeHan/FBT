Career Readiness Expo

Unlock your potential for a rewarding career in the booming renewable industry! Our comprehensive program caters to youth, adults, and participants alike, offering a targeted focus on energy efficiency, air source, geothermal heat pumps, solar, wind, and batteries.


Immerse yourself in an unparalleled, hands-on learning experience as you engage with field experts and seasoned professionals. Gain valuable insights and practical skills that will set you apart in this dynamic field.


What sets us apart? Certification and Job Placement opportunities await motivated participants. Seize the chance to not only learn but also launch your career in the renewable industry. Sign up now to secure your spot and take the first step towards a brighter, sustainable future!
